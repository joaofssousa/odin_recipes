Pasta alla Vodka

Pasta alla Vodka, its origins are unclear and debated between Italy and the US.

The dish is praised for its creamy, satisfying sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 300g penne
  • salt
  • butter
  • 2 garlic
  • 30ml vodka
  • 200g milk cream
  • 1l water
  • olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 100g pealed tomato
  • 100g parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon calabrian pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water to taste and set aside.
  2. In a pan, add the olive oil, butter, and onion. Saut√©.
  3. Add the garlic, pepper, vodka, tomato, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and basil leaves (set aside a few leaves for garnish).
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce.
  5. Finish with the basil leaves and serve.
